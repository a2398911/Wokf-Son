    Our Story

    RAISED BY WOLVES

    Dedicated to all men

    Wolf&Son is a traditional brand of men’s footwear, proudly enhanced by a junior line.
    Established on decades of knowledge it’s a label rooted on family heritage and quality craftmanship.

    A loyal brand for all the noble pack leaders, who leave their mark as they walk the path.

    Wolf＆Son是傳統品牌的男士鞋類，從初級系列一直在提升品質與用料，對於自身品牌相當地自豪。
    憑藉數十年的知識與專業所創造的作品，是高質量工藝的標籤與藝術。

    所有貴族領導者的忠實品牌，他們在走成功的道路上留下了屬於自己的印記。

    THE MAKING OF
    Wolf & Son Production

    Take a look behind the scenes at our production process in location at the Portuguese manufactory, based on the northern region of the country. Allying the virtue of craft and excellence of skill, Wolf & Son denotes careful selection to the choice of raw materials for implementation.

    Focusing on natural elements and a timeless aesthetic, Wolf & Son speaks to the contemporary male. Showing the minimum of decoration and paying the utmost attention to details, to make a long lasting shoe for all wolves and wolf cubs from the lines: Classic, Signature, Casual, Sport.
